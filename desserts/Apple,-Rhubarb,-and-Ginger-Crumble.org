* Apple, Rhubarb, and Ginger Crumble
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: apple-rhubarb-and-ginger-crumble
  :END:

** Ingredients
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: ingredients
   :END:

- apples
- rhubarb
- demerara sugar
- crumble mixture
- stem ginger pieces
- stem ginger syrup
- mascarpone cheese
- ground cinnamon
- icing sugar

** Method
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: method
   :END:

1. stew the fruit with stem ginger pieces finely chopped
2. add ginger syrup to fruit and place in oven proof dish
3. top fruit with crumble
4. bake until golden brown
5. mix mascarpone cheese with ground cinnamon and icing sugar to taste
