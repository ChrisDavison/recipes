# Soy, Sesame, and Ginger Salad Dressing

Ingredients

-   1 tbsp olive oil
-   1 tbsp sesame oil
-   1 tbsp soy sauce
-   1 tbsp maple syrup
-   1 tbsp rice vinegar
-   1 clove minced garlic
-   1 tsp minced ginger

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Method

1.  whisk together the liquids
2.  add minced ginger and garlic, and stir together till combined
