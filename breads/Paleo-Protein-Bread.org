* Paleo Protein Bread
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: paleo-protein-bread
  :END:

Makes 1 1kg loaf (2lb); 20 slices of 50g (~200cal)

** Ingredients
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: ingredients
   :END:

- 100g pumpkin seeds
- 100g sunflower seeds
- 100g almonds
- 100g walnuts
- 100g linseed
- 100g sesame seeds
- 2tsp salt
- 5 eggs
- 1 decilitre oil

** Method
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: method
   :END:

Need approx 1-2 eggs per 100g of nuts/grains/seeds.

1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Put ingredients into a bread form (lubricated if not non-stick)
3. Bake for 1 hour at 160C (for 1 or 2 litre bread form)
