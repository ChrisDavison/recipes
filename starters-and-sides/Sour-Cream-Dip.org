* Sour Cream Dip
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: sour-cream-dip
  :END:

** Ingredients
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: ingredients
   :END:

- 150ml crème fraiche
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
- freshly ground black pepper

** Method
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: method
   :END:

1. combine the crème fraiche and the chives
2. season with black pepper to taste
