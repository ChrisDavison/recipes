# Kidney-bean Salad

Ingredients

-   1 can kidney beans
-   1 can sweetcorn
-   1/2 cucumber
-   100g feta
-   50g coriander
-   50g green onions

Dressing

-   1/2 lime (juiced)
-   2tbsp olive oil
-   1tsp mustard
-   1 clove garlic
-   1-2 tsp cumin/ground
-   1/2tsp oregano
-   salt and pepper to taste

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Method

-   Rinse and drain corn and kidney beans.
-   Dice cucumber. Slice coriander
-   Chop green onion
-   Mix in everything so far. Crumble over feta.
-   Mix the dressing, and then mix in to the salad.
