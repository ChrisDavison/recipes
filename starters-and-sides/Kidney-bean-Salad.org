* Kidney-bean Salad
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: kidney-bean-salad
  :END:

** Ingredients
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: ingredients
   :END:

- 1 can kidney beans
- 1 can sweetcorn
- 1/2 cucumber
- 100g feta
- 50g coriander
- 50g green onions

Dressing

- 1/2 lime (juiced)
- 2tbsp olive oil
- 1tsp mustard
- 1 clove garlic
- 1-2 tsp cumin/ground
- 1/2tsp oregano
- salt and pepper to taste

** Method
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: method
   :END:

- Rinse and drain corn and kidney beans.
- Dice cucumber. Slice coriander
- Chop green onion
- Mix in everything so far. Crumble over feta.
- Mix the dressing, and then mix in to the salad.
