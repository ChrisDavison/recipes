# Overnight Oats

Makes 5 portions, and will keep in fridge for about 5 days

Per portion (not including toppings):

    480 cal
    36g protein
    52g carbs
    13g fat

## Ingredients

- 350g oats
- 500g yoghurt
- 125g protein powder
- 50g chia seeds
- 500ml milk

## Method

***JUST MIX IT.***

Top with banana and peanut butter, or whatever fresh fruit preference.
